How to Play

1. Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors.

2. If playing against computer, computer will randomly pick.

3. Player1 vs Player2: each player selects their choice.

4. Winner is decided based on game rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.

5. Each game lasts 1 minute; the scoreboard shows total scores.

6. Enjoy the neon game and listen to sounds (click, win, lose, draw).
